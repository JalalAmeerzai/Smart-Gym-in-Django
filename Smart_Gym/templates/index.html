What is Smart Gym?


S mart Gym is a cloud based solution for your gym related tasks, it comes bundled with all the neccessary features that are required to manage your users, staff and the equipment with ease.

As renovating is taking over all the systems and digitalizing helps to improve in so many particular ways. The Gym Management System is one of the systems which helps the management in the fast-moving digital age to tackle the tasks with minimal time consupmtion while reducing the complications that are found in traditional ways.



Module (A)
Business Website

A resposnive user-friendly webiste for the web presence of your business. It will consist of pages for your contact informations, user login, classes and events happening at your gym.

Module (B)
Admin/Users App

Being a complete solution, it also comes bundled with different dashboard apps for the admin and for the users as well. These panels are feature-rich for managing accounts and other several activities.

A mobile application is also provided for the memebrs of the gym. It is a smart app that makes your workout routine efficient and it has a built in instructor for your help.
